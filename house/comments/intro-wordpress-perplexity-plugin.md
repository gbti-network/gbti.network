We built the Perplexity Plugin to give your readers instant AI-powered insights without leaving your WordPress posts. When someone highlights text, they get a floating button to analyze it with Perplexity AI. It is a small thing that turns reading into exploration. Whether your visitors are on mobile or desktop, the plugin handles it seamlessly. This one is for content creators who want their audience to go deeper with what they are reading.
