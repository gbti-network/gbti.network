We built Travelers Lectern to help server creators easily distribute books and information across their worlds. Whether you are running a quest hub, information center, or educational server, this mod lets you place lecterns that automatically regenerate books after a configurable cooldown. It is a simple but powerful way to guide players and keep information accessible to everyone throughout your adventure.
