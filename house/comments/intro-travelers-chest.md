We built Travelers Chest to solve a problem we saw on Minecraft servers: how do you maintain balanced loot distribution without constant admin work? This mod creates chests that automatically refill on a schedule you set, giving server admins the tools to build adventure maps, RPG worlds, and resource hubs that feel alive and dynamic. Whether you are running a community server or designing adventure content, this is for anyone who wants refillable chests without the manual labor.
