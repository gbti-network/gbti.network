Most AI writing gives itself away with the same handful of patterns: the "it is not X, it is Y" reframe, the self-posed rhetorical question, the rule-of-three pileup, the em-dash habit, and the rest. This persona names them all and bans them. Paste it in front of any drafting task, or hand it a finished draft and ask it to audit against the list. It will not make a model a good writer on its own, but it strips out the tells that make text read as machine-made. The trope catalog draws on the excellent reference at [tropes.fyi](https://tropes.fyi/tropes-md). Credit to that project for cataloging the tells.
