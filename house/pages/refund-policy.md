# Refund Policy

Thank you for being part of the GBTI Network. This page explains how membership billing, cancellation, and refunds work. It sits alongside our [Terms of Service](/terms/).

## Membership and billing

- Membership starts with a 90-day, limited-access trial of the community. No payment card is required for the trial.
- Paid membership is $150 USD per year, billed through Stripe, our payment processor. GBTI does not store card details on its own systems.
- Your annual membership renews automatically unless you cancel.

## Refunds

- The trial is free, so there is nothing to refund.
- You can cancel a paid membership at any time, which stops the next renewal. You keep access for the remainder of the period you have already paid for.
- Except where required by applicable consumer law, fees already paid for the current membership period are non-refundable. If you believe your situation warrants a refund, please [contact us](/contact/) and we will review it.

## Canceling your membership

You can manage or cancel your membership from your [account page](/account/), which links to the Stripe billing portal for the email address you signed up with. Canceling stops future renewals; you are not charged again after you cancel.

## Support

Membership covers access to the community and to the perks described on the [membership page](/membership/). Direct product support is not guaranteed. Members can ask for help in the community, and helpful documentation ships with many products. Supplementary development services may be available through third-party agencies, where you can request a free estimate for work the GBTI team does not schedule.

## Contact us

Questions about billing, cancellation, or refunds can be sent through our [contact page](/contact/). We aim to respond within a reasonable time.
