Here at the [GBTI Network](https://gbti.network) our [members](https://gbti.network/members/) are building new assets _all the time_ that need a home for publishing. Being that the GBTI network is a co-op that shares revenue, including ad and sponsorship revenue, our community is the perfect place to host and promote new and novel projects.

One of the recent projects published is our [Email Signature Generator](https://gbti.network/utilities/email-signature-generator/), which offers six style variations, dynamic field population, a large host of 25 different social network icons, as well as customizable profile pictures, company logos, and for our signature that includes a banner, a banner image.

Why build this? We’ll because _we can_ and we need projects like this to practice our skillsets. Also we want others to find value in our network and our work.

We’re very pleased with the way this one turned out and we invite you to [give it a try](https://gbti.network/utilities/email-signature-generator/).
