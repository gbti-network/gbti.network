## Paid Generators

### ✉️ClickySignature.com

Clicky Signature is a service that lets businesses and individuals create **animated, interactive HTML email signatures** that embed directly into your email client with one click. You fill in your personal or company details, social links, and branding; Clicky then generates a signature that tracks analytics (clicks, engagement, trends) and supports team/department management for consistency across users. It integrates with CRMs and many existing tools, and offers both free and paid plans so you can start without credit card and scale up for more features.

### ✉️WiseStamp.com

WiseStamp.com provides a comprehensive platform for managing company wide signatures for multiple employees. This paid service requires the user to create master templates and then manually add their employees, generating a signature for each employee.  
  
Key features include customizable templates, fonts, colors, images, and social icons, plus integration with Gmail, Outlook, and other platforms. For teams, it offers centralized management, directory sync, and automatic updates. Marketing tools like banners, call-to-action buttons, and click analytics turn signatures into a lightweight channel for promotion and engagement.

### ✉️BulkSignature.com

BulkSignature is a platform for creating, deploying, and managing email signatures across an entire organization—especially for users of Google Workspace and Microsoft 365.  
  
Its features include a centralized dashboard for building and updating branded signature templates, automatic syncing of user data from directory services, and the ability to assign signatures by department, group, or individual. It supports campaign banners with scheduling and click tracking, offers role-based permissions, ensures consistency across devices and platforms, and provides an interface that doesn’t require coding for basic use.

### ✉️Signature.email

Signature.email is an email signature generator designed for teams and power users. It offers a visual drag-and-drop editor to build HTML signatures, with control over which fields are editable or locked. The platform supports consistent branding by letting you share a signature generator link with your team, ensuring everyone uses the correct template. It works across many email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, Thunderbird, etc.).

In terms of business features, Signature.email lets you define multiple signature templates (for different teams or departments), manage permissions, and push updates centrally. It also supports pricing tiers for organizations of different sizes, offering more templates, users, and administration in higher plans.
