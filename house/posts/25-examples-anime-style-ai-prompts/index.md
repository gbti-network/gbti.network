In this article, we will be sharing our breakdown of the major anime styles and how they can be prompted when generating AI images.

To help with our research, we chose to generate a classic phrase typically used in font demonstrations:

**_“A quick brown fox jumps over a lazy dog.”_**

For all anime-style image examples in this article, we’ve used the **ChatGPT o4-mini** model. Performance of the prompts below might be different when used on other models like Claude, Grok, and Gemini.
